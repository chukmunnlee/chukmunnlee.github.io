NUS-ISS has all the fundamentals to lead the movement where we create an eco-system to help build the tech caliber of Tech Startups. We are poised to amalgamate the disciplines using a 'whole-stack' approach to co-create the next engine of growth in Singapore with technologists for technologists.

StackUp’s curriculum is conceived to transform individuals into aspiring technologists. It will equip students with the foundational skills required to deliver tech products from idea through to implementation and deployment.

All StackUp’s courses are practical with emphasis on hands-on exercises and training. They are designed to help startups and SMEs to leverage on open technologies in their business operations. The programme offers the chance to participate in class, immerse in apprenticeships and apply newly-acquired principles and concepts to real-life projects.
